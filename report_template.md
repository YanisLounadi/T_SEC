## Executive Summary

High-level incident overview covering business impact, key findings, and critical recommendations.

## Investigation & Technical Analysis

### Technical Incident Overview

Summary of the case.

### Scope & Methodology

Systems investigated, tools used, forensic approach taken. and evidence sources examined.

### Detailed Findings

Detailed step-by-step investigation and analysis of artifacts, IOCs, attack vectors, and evidence collected.

### Root Cause Analysis

Identification of the initial entry point and conditions that enabled the compromise.

### Indicators of Compromise

Complete list of IoCs (IPs, domains, file hashes, registry keys, etc.).

### Attack Chain Reconstruction

Timeline of attacker actions mapped to MITRE ATT&CK framework.

## Impact Analysis

Assessment of affected systems, data exposure, operational disruption, and potential business or legal consequences.

## Remediation & Recommendations

Prioritized improvements and measures aimed at strengthening detection and response capabilities.

## Appendices

Supporting evidence such as raw logs, IOC lists, forensic hashes, tool outputs, malware samples, chain-of-custody documentation, and additional technical artifacts.
